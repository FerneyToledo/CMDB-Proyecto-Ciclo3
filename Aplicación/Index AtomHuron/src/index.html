Services

Data Base

As the owner of your own CMDB you will be able to select how you want you items to interact with themselves, also you can select people to check for them as you will see in the next service

Administrator

Select someone, could be from your own companny, an specially designated person to check for your data, this person will have the power to make changes and move freely through your Data Base

Applications

Add Apps, develop and add them to your data base, so you can feel a true inmersive experience in your own DB there could be for item interaction, development, or even organize your data, feel free to experiment
